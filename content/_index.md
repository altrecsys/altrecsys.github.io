AltRecSys is a series of workshops at the [ACM Conference on Recommender Systems][RecSys] (RecSys)
intended to host conversations that challenge conventional thinking, question fundamental assumptions,
present unusual or negative results, and otherwise advance the recommender systems research and practice conversation in highly novel directions.

- [Second workshop at RecSys 2026](recsys26/)
- [First workshop at RecSys 2024](recsys24/)
